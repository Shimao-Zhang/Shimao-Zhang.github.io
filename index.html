About me

I'm a graduate student of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University now and I have just received my B.Sc. degree in 2023. I have finished a period of unforgettable time in LAMDA Group supervised by Prof. Lijun Zhang during my undergraduate years, where I got effective basic research training. Additionally, I'm admitted to pursue my M.Sc. degree in Nanjing University without entrance examination. I'm now a member of NJU NLP Group under the supervision of Prof. Shujian Huang.

Planning to pursue my Ph.D. degree in America in several years.

Just feel free to follow my social media accounts listed in the menu if you're interested in me or my works. And if you are interested to cooperate with me, please contact with me.
